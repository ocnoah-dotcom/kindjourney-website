Privacy Policy

Effective and last updated: July 14, 2026

1. About this policy

This Privacy Policy explains how KindJourney collects, uses, and protects information when you visit kindjourney.app, join the early-access list, or contact us.

2. Information we collect

At this pre-launch stage, we may collect:

  • Your email address when you voluntarily join the early-access list.
  • Information you choose to include when you email or otherwise contact us.
  • Basic technical information that a website or hosting provider may process, such as IP address, browser type, device type, and access time.

Please do not send medical records or other sensitive health information through the website or by email.

3. How we use information

We may use the information to send KindJourney news, early-access invitations, and wellness resources; respond to questions; operate and protect the website; and understand general website performance.

4. Email choices

You may unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time by using the unsubscribe option in an email or by contacting us. We will honor valid unsubscribe requests as required by applicable law.

5. Sharing information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help us host the website, manage email signups, deliver communications, or protect the service. We may also disclose information when required by law or when reasonably necessary to protect rights, safety, and security.

6. Data retention and security

We keep personal information only for as long as reasonably needed for the purposes described here or to meet legal obligations. We use reasonable safeguards, but no online system can be guaranteed completely secure.

7. Children’s privacy

KindJourney is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted information, please contact us so we can review and delete it when appropriate.

8. External links

The website may link to social media or other third-party services. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as KindJourney develops. The revised page will show a new “last updated” date. Material changes may also be announced through the website or our official social channels.

10. Contact us

For privacy questions or requests, email kindjourneyapp@gmail.com.
